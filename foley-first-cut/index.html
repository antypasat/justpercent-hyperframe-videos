How much tip should I leave based on the bill total?

Percentage of a Number

Change the values below to see the calculation update in real time.

The restaurant bill is $150. I want to leave a 10% tip... How much should I leave?
Answer: The tip should be $15.
Round calculations to 2 decimal places
Calculation Details

Type of calculation: Calculating a percentage of a number.

How to use the calculator: Use the Basic Percentage CALCULATOR ("What is X% of Y?"). Enter the tip percentage as 10 [X] and the bill amount as 150 [Y]. It will calculate 10% of 150.

The formula: We want to find 10% of 150.
Formula: (Percentage / 100) × Whole
Tip amount = (10 / 100) × 150 = 0.1 × 150 = 15
