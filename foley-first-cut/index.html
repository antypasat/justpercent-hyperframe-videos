What's my car worth after depreciation?

New value after decrease

Change the values below to see the calculation update in real time.

My new car depreciates by about 15% in its first year. I bought it for $80000... What will it be worth after a year?
Answer: The car will be worth $68000 after one year.
Round calculations to 2 decimal places
Calculation Details

Calculation type: Finding the value after a percentage decrease.

Option 1 (Direct method): Use the Value Decrease Calculator ("Calculate new value after percentage decrease"). Enter $80000 [Original] and 15% [Decrease]. The calculator will directly give you the car's value after one year.

Formula explanation: We want to find the car's value after one year, given it loses 15% of its value from $80000.
Depreciation = (15 / 100) × $80000 = 0.15 × $80000 = $12000
Value after one year = $80000 - $12000 ≈ $68000
