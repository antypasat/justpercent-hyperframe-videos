## Style Prompt

Monochrome code-intelligence launch film for Linear: absolute black canvas, charcoal product surfaces, white typography, blurred TypeScript depth layers, and precise geometric UI. The visual world is a quiet diagnostic instrument waking up, then diving through product behavior in macro Z-space before returning to a composed answer.

## Colors

- `#000000` - absolute black background
- `#0A0A0A` - near-black scene surface
- `#111111` - charcoal panel base
- `#1A1A1A` - input and modal surfaces
- `#2A2A2A` - raised controls and dividers
- `#FFFFFF` - primary type, borders, logo
- `#AAAAAA` - secondary explanatory text
- `#666666` - muted labels and query text

## Typography

- `Montserrat` for geometric UI, headings, and Linear wordmark.
- `JetBrains Mono` for code, code paths, metadata, and token labels.

## Motion

- Smooth cubic-bezier style motion through GSAP `power2.inOut`, `power3.inOut`, `sine.inOut`, and `expo.out`.
- Multi-scene transitions use push, zoom-through, focus-pull, and color-dip patterns.
- 3D sequence uses camera-scale transforms, blur shifts, and foreground focus cards to simulate macro depth of field.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not introduce chromatic neon, gradients, or colorful accents.
- Do not use stock imagery, screenshots, or decorative illustrations.
- Do not replace the product UI with generic marketing cards.
- Do not use casual rounded display typography.
- Do not let the final answer scene become a centered slide; keep it structured like a product response.
