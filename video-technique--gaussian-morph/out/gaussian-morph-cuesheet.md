  breathes. Side-chain the shimmer bed under each sub swell.
- The choir pad at the "%" hallucination (20.3–21.6) is the emotional peak of
  the film; keep everything else at least −6 dB under it.
- Captions double as optional whisper-VO; zero dead air is NOT required —
  this film wants space.
