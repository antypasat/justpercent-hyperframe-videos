# Script — JustPercent Launch Teaser

**Target:** 14-16 seconds. ~36 words. 2.4 words/sec with deliberate pauses.

**Tone:** Calm, confident, slightly playful. Lean dry, not infomercial. Voice: en-US, neutral male — Kokoro `am_michael` or similar.

---

## Narration

> Stop guessing the math.
>
> Twenty percent off — done.
>
> The tip — done.
>
> Your new salary after a raise — done.
>
> Every percentage. Instantly.
>
> Just Percent dot com.

---

## Notes

- "Just Percent" pronounced as two words (the brand). Do **not** TTS "JustPercent" as one token — it produces a wrong stress.
- "justpercent dot com" → write "Just Percent dot com" so Kokoro reads the domain as a proper sign-off.
- Each "— done" beat is a hard punctuation pause; storyboard maps a single visual reveal to each.
- Final line is the only one that should feel slightly slower — landing the brand.

## Word count

36 words. At 2.4 wps ≈ 15s with breath pauses.
